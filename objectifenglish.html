Goals

In this section, you have the possibility to access the goals concerning the traineeships. They are compulsory and represent an important part for 2nd year students of IT DUT. They can do their internship in France but also internationally.

Knowledge synthesis Professional environment Personal skills Skills to be implemented Skills to be developed Practical links
One of the objectives of the internship is allow the student to do a synthesis knowledge developed to the UIT. He must assimilate not only the skills but also the basic concepts and working methods he learned during the DUT. In this way he will be able to integrate the requirements and expertise linked to the professional environment.
During the internship, the student must become aware of the socio-professional environment. This is his first contact with the world of work. The company will expect active production from the student. He's going to have to prove himself within the company. He doesn't have to take it as a test he could have taken at the academy.
Enabling the student to identify his or her personal abilities is also an objective of the internships. It’ is a necessary step in the construction of your student's professional project. It is the qualities and abilities of the student that will impress the recruiters he or she meets. The in-depth work, Personal and Professional Project, followed at the IUT, has already allowed the student to reflect on his potential skills. The internship, thanks to the precision of his personal skills, will allow him to progress in the construction of his professional project.
The student, thanks to his internship, will be able to implement the skills he had to acquire in his training. He must be able to :
  • Analyze, design and realize an IT solution, a website ;
  • Administering an information system, a database, networks ;
  • Advise and technically assist users, customers and services ;
  • Working in a team, communicating (in French and English) ;
  • Develop project management skills: time management, assignment distribution, autonomy.
The internship must help the student to develop skills that will be useful in his or her future professional life. He have to put into practice knowledge and know-how (research, proposal of solutions, realization, etc.) during his internship. When he will be in the company, he will be solicited and will have to be productive: it is necessary that he knows how to implement the knowledge acquired during his DUT. Within the company, he will have to develop relational skills such as autonomy, initiative, conflict management…
For more information on internships, you can visit the site of the IUT of Lens of the PPN concerning the IT DUT.
